# Technical Talent Scouting & Identification
### Moving Beyond Databases to Market Mapping

Standard recruitment relies on "Post and Pray" (job boards) or keyword matching. My methodology is built on **Technical Signal Identification**—locating the engineers who are building the future of the industry in private or specialized circles.

## 🛠️ The 3-Layer Identification Process:

### 1. Digital Footprint Analysis (The "Scent")
I track engineers through their actual output, not just their resumes:
* **Open Source Contribution:** Identifying core contributors to high-performance libraries (Rust, Go, JVM).
* **Technical Writing:** Tracking engineers who publish deep-dives on distributed systems or kernel-level optimization.
* **Specialized Forums:** Monitoring high-signal discussions in niche engineering communities.

### 2. Market Mapping (The "Network")
I map the "Engineering Lineage" of top-tier firms (e.g., Jane Street, Cloudflare, Netflix) to identify:
* **Talent Incubators:** Teams known for producing "Metal" level systems experts.
* **Succession Tracks:** Identifying the "Rising Stars" working under world-class Architects.

### 3. The Consultative Engagement
I approach talent as a **Technical Peer**. I don't sell "jobs"; I discuss **Architectural Challenges** and **System Constraints** to find the perfect alignment between a candidate's mastery and a company's mission.
